About Nicholas

Nicholas began his Real Estate career with a second chance apartment-locating firm in 2008. Within seven months, he joined a fast-paced brokerage in the Uptown neighborhood. He remained with this company for the next six years, where he achieved the experience needed to succeed in this cutthroat market. In 2015 he joined Martin-Sena & Associates, llc where he has continued to prevail in the Dallas Real Estate market!

He's carved his own niche in the Downtown market, leasing high-end real estate, one-of-a-kind industrial loft spaces while also juggling listings in both the residential sales market as well as the commercial side of the business.

In his spare time, he enjoys socializing throughout the City, including trying new eateries, dines & entertainment hot-spots. He remains eextremly and is a member of the Dallas Running Club and is currently training for a marathon. Nick has been a resident in Downtown Dallas since 2009, reaffirming his admiration and knowledge of the area!
